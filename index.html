About 180 Degrees Student Consulting Munich

As the world's largest student consultancy, 180 DC supports non-profit organizations and social entrepreneurs to master strategic challenges. Based on our know-how and comprehensive consulting experience, we develop tailor-made, conceptual, and innovative solutions together with our project partners. Thus, we create a decisive social impact on business and society.
Our Munich branch was named Best Global Branch 2018, Most Innovative Branch 2019, and Best EMEA Branch 2020 out of more than 150 180DC branches worldwide.
